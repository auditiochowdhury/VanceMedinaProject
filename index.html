Hello, I'm Vance

I am an artist based in New Jersey. Through dynamic brushwork and a juxtaposition of contrasting concepts, I explore the intricate balance between light and darkness, chaos and serenity, and life and death. Drawing inspiration from the raw emotions within, I layer bold textures with mediums like acrylic paints, charcoal, and ink, resulting in thought-provoking compositions. Ultimately, my art aims to evoke profound contemplation and ignite dialogues on the complexities of the human experience
